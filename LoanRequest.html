Book or Media Request

<#INCLUDE filename='include_request_cited.html'> <#INCLUDE filename='include_request_buttons.html'>

All Interlibrary Loan book/media requests are presumed to be for physical materials. Typically, physical books and other items arrive at the library within one to two weeks after you place your request although some may take longer.

If only a digital format is wanted, please make a note on your request. eBooks are not often available via Interlibrary Loan, but we will explore these options.
